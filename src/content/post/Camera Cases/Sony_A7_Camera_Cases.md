### What's the difference between a Sony A7 camera case and a holster?

A camera case is designed to store and protect your camera and its accessories, whereas a holster is specifically meant for carrying and securing your camera on your belt or bag. Holsters typically provide quick access to your camera but may not offer as much protection as a full-sized case.
