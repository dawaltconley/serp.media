## FAQ


### What is a Spy Camera Phone Case?

A Spy Camera Phone Case is a protective case for your smartphone that also features a hidden camera. These cases allow you to discretely capture images or videos without others noticing that a recording is taking place. 



### How do I install the camera on my phone?

The camera installation varies depending on the manufacturer's instructions. Generally, it involves connecting the camera module to your phone via USB or micro-USB. Follow the instructions carefully and ensure compatibility with your phone model before installation. 


### How do I operate the camera in the phone case?

Operation of the camera depends on the design of the case. Typically, there will be a button or touch sensor on the case that allows you to capture images or videos. Some cases may also feature an app for controlling the camera remotely. 



### How can I view the recorded footage?

To view the recorded footage, you will need to remove the memory card from the camera phone case and insert it into a computer or another device with a compatible card reader. 


### How long does the battery last?

Battery life varies depending on the model and usage. Generally, the battery can last between one to three hours when actively recording. Keeping the case charged at all times ensures optimal battery performance. 



### Do these cases work with all smartphone models?

No, not all models are compatible with these cases. Ensure that you choose a camera phone case designed specifically for your smartphone model to guarantee proper installation and functioning. 


### How do I know if the camera is recording?

Many spy camera phone cases come with indicator lights that show when the camera is active. Additionally, some models have apps that allow you to monitor the camera's status remotely. 



### Are these cases legal to use?

The legality of using spy camera phone cases varies by location and purpose. In some jurisdictions, it may be illegal to record someone without their consent. Before using such a case, make sure to research your local laws and obtain any necessary permits or permissions. 


### How do I clean and maintain the camera phone case?

Clean the case with a soft, slightly damp cloth and avoid using harsh chemicals or abrasive materials. Regularly check the charging port and camera lens for dust or debris, and gently clean them using a soft brush or cloth. Keep the case stored in a protective case when not in use to prevent scratches and damage. 


### How can I troubleshoot common issues with the spy camera phone case?

* If the camera is not recording, ensure that the camera module is properly connected and powered on.

* If the battery life is shorter than expected, try replacing the battery or ensuring that the case is fully charged before use.

* If the video or image quality is poor, ensure that the camera lens is clean and free of smudges or debris.
