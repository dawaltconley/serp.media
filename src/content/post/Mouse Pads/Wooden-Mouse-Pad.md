### What size should I look for when purchasing a wooden mouse pad?

The size of a wooden mouse pad will depend on the size of your mouse and the space you have on your desk. A standard size for a mouse pad is 15 inches by 10 inches, but you can find mouse pads in larger sizes for use with gaming mice or laptops. 


### Can I use a wooden mouse pad on any surface?


Wooden mouse pads can be used on a variety of surfaces, including hardwood, tile, and laminate flooring. However, they may not work well on soft or porous surfaces, such as carpet or fabric. 


### How do I clean a wooden mouse pad?

* Dust your wooden mouse pad regularly with a soft cloth or duster to prevent dirt buildup.

* For more thorough cleaning, dampen a soft cloth with water and mild dish soap. Wipe the surface of the mouse pad with the cloth, but be sure to avoid getting any water or soap inside the pad.

* Once cleaned, let the mouse pad air dry completely before using it again.


### Is a wooden mouse pad better for my wrist than other materials?

Compared to other materials, a wooden mouse pad can provide a more comfortable surface for your wrist, as it is generally softer than plastic or metal mouse pads.
