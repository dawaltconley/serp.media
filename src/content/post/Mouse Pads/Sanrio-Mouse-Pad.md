### Material

Sanrio mouse pads typically come in two main materials: velvet or rubber. Velvet mouse pads offer a smoother surface, making them easier to glide your mouse over. Rubber mouse pads, on the other hand, provide better grip and resistance, which can be helpful if you have a faster-moving cursor or prefer a more durable surface. Consider your personal preference and usage habits when choosing between these two materials. Additionally, if you are sensitive to dust, opt for a velvet mouse pad as they tend to catch and hold less dust. 


### Size

Sanrio mouse pads are available in various sizes to fit your specific needs. Measure the space where your mouse pad will sit and compare that against the size options available. A larger mouse pad will give you more workspace, but ensure it won't cover any vital keys or buttons on your keyboard. Additionally, consider the portability factor if you need to switch from one workspace to another frequently. Smaller mouse pads may be easier to carry and transport.
