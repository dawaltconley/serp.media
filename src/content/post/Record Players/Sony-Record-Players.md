## FAQ



### What are the key features of Sony record players?

Sony record players come with a range of features including full-frequency response, anti-skating technology, and dynamic balance. These features help ensure the highest quality sound and prevent damage to the records. Some models also offer built-in speakers and the option to connect to external audio systems. Additionally, many Sony record players are designed with a vintage aesthetic, making them a stylish addition to any home. 


### What types of records can Sony record players play?


Sony record players are capable of playing various types of records, including 33 1/3, 45, and 78 RPM records. Some models even support 10-inch records. It's essential to check the specifications of the desired record player to ensure compatibility with your record collection. 


### What is the difference between a manual and automatic record player?

Manual record players require the user to manually lower and lift the tonearm to play a record, while automatic record players use sensors to detect when the grooves of the record have finished playing and automatically lift the tonearm. Automatic record players are designed for convenience and to prevent wear on records due to manual handling. 


### Are Sony record players compatible with modern audio systems?

Yes, many Sony record players come with built-in Bluetooth or USB connectivity options, allowing them to be connected to modern audio systems such as speakers, soundbars, and home theaters. This allows users to enjoy their vinyl records while also taking advantage of modern audio technology. 


### What are the benefits of owning a Sony record player?
